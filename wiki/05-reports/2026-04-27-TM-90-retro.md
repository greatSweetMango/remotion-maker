# TM-90 retro

## Went well
- TM-80's audit gave a tight, prioritized fix list — went straight to code,
  no exploration phase.
- jest-axe is plug-and-play with the existing jest+jsdom setup; no
  config changes needed.
- One `useId()` wired through the visible `<Label>` covers every control
  variant in `ParameterControl`. No per-type label plumbing.

## Friction
- shadcn primitives (Switch, Slider) don't auto-propagate accessible names
  from props to the focusable inner element. Slider in particular needed a
  type-level extension to even accept `aria-label`. Worth memorizing as a
  recurring trap when adding shadcn primitives.
- TS errors in `__tests__/benchmarks/` and `evaluator-fuzz.test.ts` are
  unrelated but pollute `tsc --noEmit` output. Should be cleaned up
  separately (target/lib config in tsconfig).
