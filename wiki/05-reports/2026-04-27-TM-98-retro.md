## Key decisions
- Bump desktop gate from `md:` (768) → `lg:` (1024) instead of patching
  individual components for tablet. One root toggle, predictable result.
- Persist `<PlayerPanel>` at top of mobile/tablet stack instead of mounting
  per-tab — eliminates the 0×0 first-paint failure mode.
